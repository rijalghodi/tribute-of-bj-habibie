Bachtiar Jusuf Habibie

The Father of Indonesian Technology

His Timeline

  • 25 Juni 1936 - He was born and has the full name Bachruddin Jusuf Habibie
  • 1954 - Studied at ITB and received a scholarship from the Minister of Education and Culture to continue his studies in Germany
  • 1955 - Studied in Germany specializing in aircraft construction at the Rhein Westfalen Aechen Techni
  • 1960 - Habibie earned a diploma in engineering with cum laude predicate. Five years later, he received his doctorate in engineering with the predicate summa cum laude. Overall, he spent 10 years studying in Germany
  • 1973 - Habibie was trusted to be the first leader of PT. Industri Pesawat Terbang Negara (The State Aircraft Industry Company), which has now changed its name to PT. Dirgantara Indonesia
  • 1978 - Habibie was appointed minister of research and technology for two decades
  • 1995 - Habibie successfully launched a test flight of the N250 aircraft, the first aircraft made by the Indonesian people, on 10 August
  • 1998 - On March 11, 1998, Habibie officially became the 7th vice president of Indonesia. During the 1998 crisis, Habibie served as president of the Republic of Indonesia, replacing Suharto. Under his leadership Indonesia managed to survive the crisis
  • 2019 - He passed away on Wednesday 11 September 2019 at 18:05 at Gatot Subroto Army Hospital
